About Feathered Greeters

Welcome to Feathered Greeters, your premier destination for professional ushering and party decoration services in Entebbe, Uganda. Located within the vibrant community of Nkumba University, we specialize in making your special occasions truly memorable.

Our passion lies in creating beautiful, welcoming environments for all types of celebrations. Whether you're planning an intimate wedding, a grand birthday celebration, or a corporate event, our experienced team brings creativity, professionalism, and attention to detail to every project.

We understand that every event is unique, and we work closely with our clients to bring their vision to life. From elegant floral arrangements to sophisticated color schemes, from professional ushering services to complete event coordination, we handle every aspect with care and precision.
